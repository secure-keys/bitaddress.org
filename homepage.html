How To Use Bitaddress To Encrypt An Existing Bitcoin Wallet

On the standard generator app, select Wallet Details from the tab, and enter your private key or master seed directly in the input field, check the "BIP38 Encrypt" box and set a BIP38 decryption password, then click "Encrypt BIP38".

For wallet generation usage, select the desired type from the menu tab in the standard generator app.

Note that the wallet generation options—'Single Wallet', 'Paper Wallet', 'Bulk Wallet', 'Vanity Wallet', and 'Split Wallet'—rely on a process called entropy generation, which increases the randomness of your generated keys. Once the seeder level reaches 100%, the tabs will become clickable, allowing you to proceed with your wallet generation safely. Move your mouse around or touch anywhere on your mobile phone screen to increase the seeder %.

If you do not have the patience to move your mouse or tap your mobile phone screen until the seeder pool reaches 100%, you can use our advanced lightning⚡️ version designed for maximum entropy generation with just a single touch. Click the button below.
