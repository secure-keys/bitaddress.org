Privacy Policy for BitAddressApp

Last Updated: 10/15/24

1. Introduction

Welcome to BitAddressApp, an open-source client-side Bitcoin wallet generator. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app. By using BitAddressApp, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

No Personal Information: We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII), such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

No Usage Data: We do not collect any non-personal information about your device, including IP addresses, browser types, or usage patterns.

No Logs: There are no logs of user activity or interactions with the app. Your privacy is our priority.


3. How the App Works

BitAddressApp operates entirely on your device. All processes, including wallet generation and QR code creation, occur locally without transmitting any data to external servers. This ensures that your information remains confidential and secure.
