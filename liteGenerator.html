Only private keys are generated using this tool. If you want to fund your wallet, import your generated Private key into a wallet app. A wallet address will be assigned to your private key. Use that wallet address to receive Bitcoin from any source. You can then delete the imported private key/wallet from the wallet app if you so wish. Your funds will remain in your bitcoin wallet. To check your balance or deposit/withdraw your Bitcoin, repeat the importing process.
